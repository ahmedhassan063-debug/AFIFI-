Quick answers about sizing, delivery, exchanges, and how to place an order with AFIFI.

FAQ

How do I place an order?

Add the product to your cart, then send the order on WhatsApp. You can also contact us directly with the product name and size.

Are the products original?

AFIFI focuses on original brand selections and clear product details. Ask us on WhatsApp if you need extra confirmation before ordering.

Can I reserve an item?

Reservations depend on stock availability. Message us with the product name, size, and color and we will confirm.

Shipping & Returns

Cairo: Usually 2-3 business days.

Outside Cairo: Usually 4-6 business days.

Exchange: Available within 14 days if the item is unused and kept in its original condition.

Returns: Contact us first so we can confirm the case and guide you through the steps.
