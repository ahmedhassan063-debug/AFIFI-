OUR STORY

Crafted in Egypt, Worn Worldwide

AFIFI was born in 2021 from a simple belief: that Egyptian craftsmanship deserves a global stage. What started as a small workshop in Cairo has grown into a brand that celebrates our heritage through modern streetwear.

Every piece we create tells a story of quality, passion, and the relentless pursuit of perfection. We source the finest Egyptian cotton, work with skilled local artisans, and design with the modern individual in mind.
